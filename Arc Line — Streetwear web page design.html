YNG_Official — Langa

Role: Lead Visual Designer & Urban Concept Artist

Langa creates the visual language of Arc Line: character silhouettes, graffiti motifs, and the signature dancing figure that appears across our drops. He mixes local textures and township art references with bold typographic systems.

About Arc Line

Founded in 2025 in South Africa, Arc Line bridges street culture and performance art. Our collections are limited drops — designed by YNG_Official and LMN_MusiQ — and produced with a focus on ethics, quality, and community collaboration.
